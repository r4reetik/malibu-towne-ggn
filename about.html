Welcome to Malibu Towne, an integrated township

Malibu Towne is a one of a kind development and is amongst one of the most beautiful townships in the National Capital Region (NCR). Malibu Estate Pvt. Ltd., the parent company responsible for the development of Malibu Towne has employed many professionals from the real estate industry, who are experts in their respective fields of work.

Integrated Township Project, Malibu Towne is located on the main Sohna Road (6 lane), Malibu Towne is only 2 km. from Rajiv Chowk on the new NH8 expressway and is only a 30-minute drive from the New Delhi Indira Gandhi International Airport. Spread over 200 acres of prime freehold land and Malibu Towne is an American-style Gated township.

Project Overview

Malibu Towne is spread over 200 acres of prime freehold land and enjoys all the practical comforts expected of a modern day township. The grand entrance of Malibu Towne and the initial drive into the township is unique in itself, inspiring within you, a feeling of awe and excitement at first sight. The township was launched in the year 1994 and is currently home to over 1500 families.

Designed along the lines of a typical American gated community, Malibu Towne has been built on the strict fundamentals of providing a serene surrounding enriched by extensive green areas, several culde- sacs ensuring no traffic flow through, multi-tier security system to ensure peace of mind and, last if not least, a clean environment. Malibu Towne charms you with its style and beauty in plenty.
